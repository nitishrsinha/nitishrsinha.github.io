Welcome

I am a Group Manager of the Financial Projections Group at the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System. My research focuses on macroeconomics, artificial intelligence applications in economic research, and financial markets analysis.

In my current role, I lead a team of economists and research assistants in forecasting key financial variables, guiding Board-wide model developments including AI initiatives, and briefing Board members on financial conditions.

  • Natural Language Processing

  • Text Analysis in Finance
