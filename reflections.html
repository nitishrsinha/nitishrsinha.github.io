Text Analysis in Finance

Natural Language Processing in Economic Research

Exploring the application of advanced NLP techniques to analyze financial documents, policy statements, and market communications. This work focuses on extracting meaningful signals from textual data to improve economic forecasting and policy analysis.

AI Applications in Monetary Policy

Investigating how artificial intelligence can enhance traditional economic modeling and provide new insights into monetary policy transmission mechanisms. This includes the development of machine learning models for financial market analysis.

Financial Sentiment Analysis

Research on measuring and quantifying market sentiment through text analysis of financial news, earnings calls, and policy communications. This work aims to improve our understanding of how information flows affect financial markets.
